About Shree Tharmo Packaging

Established in 2017, Shree Tharmo Packaging, located in Wagholi, Pune, is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality thermocol (EPS) packaging solutions.

We specialize in thermocol boxes, moulded EPS packaging, sheets, customized models, and protective packaging for fragile and sensitive products.
Our solutions are widely used for electronics, bottles, jars, machinery parts, medical equipment, and industrial components.

Customer satisfaction and product quality are our top priorities. With advanced manufacturing techniques and strict quality control, we ensure safe, durable, and cost-effective packaging solutions for local, national, and export markets.
